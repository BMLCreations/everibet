Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 5, 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need to run EveriBet. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. You can request deletion of your account at any time.

Who We Are

EveriBet is a mobile application developed and operated by EveriBet. We built EveriBet to let friends track casual bets on anything — no real money, no sportsbooks, just bragging rights.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at bmstudioco@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We collect the following when you use EveriBet. These categories align with Apple's App Privacy nutrition labels:

Data Linked to You (associated with your account):

  • Contact Info — email address used to create and identify your account
  • Identifiers — your user ID within EveriBet
  • User Content — bets you create, accept, or participate in; group names and activity
  • Usage Data — how you interact with the app (screens visited, features used)

Data Not Linked to You (not tied to your identity):

  • Device Info — device model and OS version for compatibility
  • Diagnostics — crash logs and performance data to improve the app

Data Used to Track You (free users only, requires ATT permission):

  • Advertising Data — advertising identifier (IDFA) used by Google AdMob to serve personalized ads. This data is only collected if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or any data outside of normal app usage.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your EveriBet account
  • To facilitate bets between you and your friends
  • To send in-app notifications about your bets
  • To maintain your win/loss record and bet history
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We apply row-level security to ensure users can only access data they are permitted to see.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

In-App Purchases

EveriBet offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock premium features. All payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We never see or store your payment information.

Advertising

EveriBet displays ads through Google AdMob to support the free version of the app. Users who purchase a subscription have ads completely removed — AdMob is never loaded for subscribers and no advertising data is collected from them.

For free users, AdMob may collect the following information to serve ads:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) — a unique device identifier used to personalize ads
  • IP address — used to infer approximate location for ad targeting
  • Device information — model, operating system version, and screen size
  • App usage data — which ads are shown, viewed, or tapped
  • Inferred interests — based on behavior across apps and services using Google's ad network

When you first open EveriBet, you will be prompted by Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework to allow or deny ad tracking. You are free to choose either option:

  • Allow tracking — personalized ads based on your interests and behavior
  • Deny tracking — non-personalized ads only. AdMob will still collect IP address and device info but will not use your IDFA

You can change this permission at any time in your iPhone's Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → EveriBet.

For more information on how Google uses data in advertising, visit Google's Advertising Policy.

No Real Money Gambling

EveriBet is a social bet tracking app. No real money is wagered, transferred, or processed through the app at any time. All bets are informal agreements between users and carry no monetary value within the platform.

Children's Privacy

EveriBet is intended for users 17 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.
