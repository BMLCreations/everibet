Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 5, 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need to run EveriBet. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. You can request deletion of your account at any time.

Who We Are

EveriBet is a mobile application developed and operated by BML Creations. We built EveriBet to let friends track casual bets on anything — no real money, no sportsbooks, just bragging rights.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at bmstudioco@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We collect the following when you use EveriBet:

  • Email address and username when you create an account
  • Bets you create, accept, or participate in
  • Friends and groups you interact with in the app
  • Device type and OS version for compatibility purposes
  • In-app notification preferences

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, or any data outside of normal app usage.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your EveriBet account
  • To facilitate bets between you and your friends
  • To send in-app notifications about your bets
  • To maintain your win/loss record and bet history
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We apply row-level security to ensure users can only access data they are permitted to see.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

In-App Purchases

EveriBet offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock premium features. All payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We never see or store your payment information.

No Real Money Gambling

EveriBet is a social bet tracking app. No real money is wagered, transferred, or processed through the app at any time. All bets are informal agreements between users and carry no monetary value within the platform.

Children's Privacy

EveriBet is intended for users 17 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at bmstudioco@gmail.com.

Third-Party Services

EveriBet uses the following third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider. Privacy Policy

  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management. Privacy Policy

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion of your account, we will permanently delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Anonymized, aggregated data (such as overall app usage statistics) may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be used to identify you.

To exercise these rights, contact us at bmstudioco@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days.
